About

Mission

Voyager Consulting is a UC Berkeley sponsored, student-run organization that provides strategy consulting services to growth-stage and Fortune 500 companies.

History

Founded in 2012, Voyager has grown to 4 project teams and nearly 30 members. In that time Voyager has completed projects for a number of leading companies, including Amazon, Instagram and the NBA. Alumni have brought those experiences to prestigious firm such as Bain & Company, Deloitte, Google and Apple.

Culture

The Voyager team is a close group of individuals bonded by shared personal and professional pursuits. New members connect over a weekend retreat and regular weekly socials such as happy hours, team dinners, concerts, kick-backs and more.

People

Voyager members hail from a diverse range of backgrounds and major including Regents Scholar, EECS, Business Administration, Economics, Rhetoric, and Public Policy. What defines a Voyager member is a desire to grow through real-world experiences through an avenue outside of Berkeley and academics.

Projects

Every semester Voyager works with clients on 4 different projects for 10-12 weeks. Projects are headed by a project leader and strategy advisor with a team of 4 to 5 consultants. Alumni and faculty mentors also play a role when appropriate.

Impact

The key to Voyager’s growth has been a tireless work ethic to support the right strategy with the appropriate data no matter the size or difficulty of the problem. Each team is built specifically around the client and strives to craft a solution that will not only inform a client decision but make a measurable impact towards a client’s goal.
